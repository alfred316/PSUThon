Our mission: to lessen the burden of textbook costs and to inspire learning, one student at a time.

With the rising cost of a college education, we strive to help Penn State students save money by selling and buying textbooks directly to one another. According to the New York Times, students on average spend $700 to $1,000 a year on textbooks. Some students even skip buying a textbook because of its cost. We’re cutting out the middleman and facilitating a learning community. It’s easy and it’s free!
